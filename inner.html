Wing-2-Wing (Wing Account to Wing Account)

(Account to Account)

Sending money to another WING account is the easiest and cheapest way of sending money in Cambodia. The money is instantly transferred from the sender's account to the receiver's account. A receipt is sent by SMS to both sides, indicating the amount of money transferred, the transaction reference ID, and the new balance.

Get Started With
Your Account

When you register with WING, you will be asked to a new PIN from your phone. You’ll need this PIN to access your WING account and complete your transaction on your mobile phone.

Choose numbers that you can easily remember and that no one else knows.

Accessing Your Wing Account

To enter your WING account and view the WING Main Menu with your mobile phone, you will need to always follow these 3 basic steps:

  • 1

    Enter *989#

  • 2

    Enter your 8 digit WING number (8 digits)

  • 3

    Enter your own PIN number (4 digits)

  • 3

    Enter your own PIN number (4 digits)

  • 3

    Enter your own PIN number (4 digits)

You will then see the WING Main Menu. From here, you can choose what you want to do.

Changing Your Wing PIN

MOBILE PHONE

How to change your Wing PIN in your mobile phone for the first time:

  1. Enter *989#
  2. Enter your WING number (8 digits)
  3. Enter the PIN number (4 digits) that you find on the scratchcard.
  4. Enter your new personal and secret PIN number.
  5. Re-enter your new personal and secret PIN number to confirm.

Then your PIN is now yours. Keep the scratchcard, it will be needed for the PIN change of the ATM card.

ATM

How to change your Wing PIN at ATM for the first time:

  1. Insert Wing card
  2. Choose Language
  3. Enter PIN
  4. Choose Other Services
  5. Choose Change PIN
  6. Enter Old PIN
  7. Enter New PIN
  8. Enter New PIN again to Confirm
  9. PIN successfully changed

Your Secret
PIN Code

It is a number made up of 4 digits. With your PIN, you can access your WING account.

Remember, your PIN allows you to transact your money in your WING account. If you give it to someone else they can access your money. So, please never share your PIN code to other, keep it safe and secret.

How to keep your secret number safe:

  • Never share your PIN with anyone.
  • Never write it down for anyone to see.
  • Never show it to anyone.
  • Never keep it with your WING Card.
